CORE FEATURES
================================================================================

🔄 REAL-TIME OPTIMIZATION (Every 30 minutes)
  Before: SMS 40% / Kakao 35% / Email 25% (고정)
  After:  SMS 45% / Kakao 38% / Email 17% (동적, 30분마다 재계산)

  Decision Rules:
  - Rule 1: SMS 개방율 > 30% → SMS 할당 +50%
  - Rule 2: Kakao ROI > Email ROI → Kakao로 예산 이동
  - Rule 3: Email 전환율 > SMS → 확인 메시지용 권장
  - Rule 4: 실패율 > 5% → 할당 감소

🧠 THOMPSON SAMPLING (Bayesian Multi-Armed Bandit)
  Algorithm: Beta(successes+α, failures+β) 분포 기반

  Traditional A/B: A vs B 50/50 test → 50% 낭비
  Thompson:       Data-driven allocation → 최소 낭비 + 수학적 수렴 보장

  Features:
  - 80% 활용(exploitation), 20% 탐색(exploration)
  - 1-2주 내 자동 수렴
  - 연락처별 개별 학습 가능

💰 AUTO BUDGET ALLOCATION (Monthly + Weekly Rebalance)
  Process:
  1. 과거 3개월 ROI 점수 계산
  2. 가중치 기반 배분
  3. A/B 테스트 5% 예약
  4. 제약 적용 (min 10%, max 60%)

  Example ($10K/month):
  SMS:   $3,500 (35%) - ROI 1.5
  Kakao: $3,500 (35%) - ROI 2.1 ← 최고
  Email: $2,500 (25%) - ROI 1.8
  A/B:   $  500 (5%)  - 테스트

⏰ OPTIMAL SEND TIME LEARNING (6-month history)
  Learns: Contact-level patterns, by day-of-week, channel-specific

  Output: "Send to John on SMS at 9:00 AM (32% open, 85% confidence)"

  Factors:
  - 개인별 시간대 패턴 (6개월 이력)
  - 요일별 변동성
  - 채널별 차이 (SMS vs Kakao vs Email)
  - 메시지 유형별 (홍보 vs 거래)

🎁 PREDICTIVE OFFER OPTIMIZATION
  8 Offer Options:
  - Discounts: 5%, 10%, 15%, 20%
  - Non-discounts: Free shipping, Trial extension, Bundle, Bonus points

  Decision Rules:
  - L1 Lens (가격 민감도): High → Big discount
  - LTV (고객 가치): VIP → Bundle/Points, Regular → 10%, New → 5%
  - Purchase Frequency: Frequent → Loyalty
  - Message Type: PROMOTIONAL (큰 할인), TRANSACTIONAL (작은 할인)

  Output: "Recommend 15% discount to Jane (82% acceptance)"

KEY INSIGHTS
================================================================================

Why 30 Minutes?
  1 minute:   Too frequent (noise)
  1 hour:     Too infrequent (slow reaction)
  30 minutes: Goldilocks (5-10 samples + 10-min processing)

Why Thompson Sampling?
  Solves Exploration-Exploitation Tradeoff:
  - Exploration: 20% random selection (find new opportunities)
  - Exploitation: 80% best-known (maximize revenue)
  - Automatic balance as confidence grows

Why 10-60% Constraints?
  Lower bound (10%):  Maintain diversity (recover from channel failure)
  Upper bound (60%):  Preserve exploration (find new channels/strategies)

Why Beta Distribution?
  Beta(α, β) = natural conjugate prior for binomial likelihoods
  → Update rule is closed-form analytical
  → No approximations needed
  → Mathematically proven convergence
