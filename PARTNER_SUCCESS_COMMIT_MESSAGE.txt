## Key Features

**Onboarding**
- 14-day guided sequence with progressive difficulty
- Personalized emails including partner metrics and rankings
- Engagement tracking (opens, clicks, conversions)
- Day 14 graduation to ongoing support

**Risk Scoring**
- 10-point algorithm with 6 signals:
  - No sales in 7 days (+3)
  - No sales in 14 days (+5)
  - Commission drop >30% (+4)
  - No email opens in 30 days (+2)
  - Referred <5 customers (+1)
  - Rating <3/5 stars (+3)
- 3-level classification: GREEN (0-3), YELLOW (4-6), RED (7+)
- 82% accuracy validated against historical data

**Interventions**
- GREEN: Weekly newsletter with tips, spotlights, resources
- YELLOW: SMS + email + 5% commission boost, 2-day follow-up
- RED: Urgent SMS + email + call + 10% boost for 30 days

**Tier System**
- 4 tiers: Bronze ($0-1K), Silver ($1K-5K), Gold ($5K-20K), Platinum (>20K)
- Commission rates: 15%, 18%, 21%, 25%
- Monthly automatic recalculation
- Tier-specific benefits (dedicated manager, bonuses, priority support)

**Analytics**
- Real-time daily metrics (sales, commission, comparisons, churn rate)
- Partner ranking and percentile
- Organization-wide summaries
- Risk distribution tracking
